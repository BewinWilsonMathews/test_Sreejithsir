A Bit About Me

Who Am I?

Sreejith S Nair ( M.Sc, M.phil (Organic Chemistry), (M.phil )(Applied Psychology)) is the founder & chairman of KBS Academy, a pioneer NEET/JEE Entrance Coaching centre in Palakkad District,Kerala.He is the past president of Junior Chamber International, Palghat &amp; won the most outstanding president for the year 2020 among three districts in Kerala. He was the most outstanding JCI member in the year 2019. He is the secretary of Lions club of Palakkad Green City in the year 2020-21. He is the Vice President of Rotary Club of Palakkad Fort in the year 2020-21. He is also a member of Palakkad Trainers Forum .
